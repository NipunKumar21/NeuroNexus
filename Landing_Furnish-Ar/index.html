FAQ

Any Questions?

Can I purchase furniture directly through the app?

Yes, Furnish-AR integrates with partner stores or e-commerce platforms, allowing you to make direct purchases of selected furniture items within the app.

How can I use Furnish-AR to place furniture in my space?

To use Furnish-AR, simply open the app, select a furniture item from the catalog, and point your device's camera to the area where you want to place the furniture. Adjust the item's position and orientation until you're satisfied with how it looks in your space.

How accurate is the sizing of furniture when placed in my room?

Furnish-AR takes accurate measurements and scaling into account to ensure the furniture appears realistically in your space. However, it's recommended to double-check measurements to ensure proper fit.

Can I try different styles or colors of furniture before making a decision?

Yes, Furnish-AR allows you to explore various styles, colors, materials, and sizes for each furniture item. Use the customization options within the app to see different variations and choose the one that best fits your preferences.
